Meet the Candidates


President

Asa

Asa Asad is a Junior majoring in Neuroscience and minoring in Logic, argumentation, and Writing. During his time in USG, Asa is most excited to work with the executive team to achieve expanded tutoring services, advocate for blind grading, and create feedback loops to advocate for incoming students.



Vice President

Dilay

Dilay Heybeli is a sophomore majoring in Industrial Engineering. As an international student herself, she is eager to work towards providing a welcoming environment to UIC students!



Student Trustee

Quinn

Quinn Basta is a Junior majoring in Finance and will serve as the Chief of Staff on the Executive Team in USG. This year, Quinn hopes to continue advocating for the student body. He believes that he has a unique opportunity to drive change to benefit all students at UIC. He is most ecstatic to have the position as Chief of Staff to continue striving for the betterment of the Undergraduate Student Government.
